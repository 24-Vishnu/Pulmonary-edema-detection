About Pulmonary Edema

Pulmonary edema is a condition caused by excess fluid in the lungs. It can be identified in chest X-rays as increased opacity or whiteness in the lung fields, often with characteristic patterns. This system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze chest X-rays and detect signs of pulmonary edema.

Note: This system is intended as a supportive tool and should not replace professional medical diagnosis.
